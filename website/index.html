Built for how real meetings run

Schedule with room and time, invite your participant book, and optionally capture audio when transcription is enabled. PortIQ keeps the thread from first invite to approved summary.

  • Scheduled notifications with .ics and one-tap calendar deep links
  • In-room flow with pause, resume, and plan-aware duration guidance
  • Follow-up meetings with checkpoint recaps emailed when you choose

From transcript to trusted deliverable

Summaries aren’t sprayed into inboxes unchecked. An authorized editor can refine copy, key points, decisions, next steps, and notes—then approve for distribution.

  • PDF minutes attached to outbound mail
  • Action items with due dates surfaced for calendar adds
  • Translated summary blocks when you need another language
